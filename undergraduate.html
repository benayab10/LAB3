Why Choose UMBC?

UMBC is recognized for its inclusive community, top-notch faculty, and dedication to student success. Here are just a few reasons students choose UMBC:

  • Small class sizes that foster personalized learning.
  • Opportunities for undergraduate research and internships.
  • Strong connections to industries and employers in the Baltimore-Washington area.
  • Vibrant campus life with diverse student organizations and events.

Facilities and Resources

UMBC provides state-of-the-art facilities and resources to support academic and professional growth:

  • Modern classrooms equipped with the latest technology.
  • Dedicated computer labs and study spaces for Information Systems students.
  • Access to online resources, including databases and journals.
  • Support services such as advising, tutoring, and career counseling.
